CAT

A cat's purr vibrates at a frequency of 25 to 150 hertz, which is the same frequency at which muscles and bones repair themselves.

  • Scientific Name: Felis catus
  • Lifespan: 12 to 16 years
  • Size: Small to medium
  • Habitat: Various environments, including homes and urban areas

Cats have endeared themselves to people all around the world as cherished animal companions. They come with a wide spectrum of personalities, from those who are naturally curious and always on the move, to those who prefer to snuggle up on a warm lap for affection. A notable aspect of cats is their exceptional self-grooming routine, displaying a level of cleanliness and care that's both fascinating and endearing. Additionally, their agility is truly remarkable, as they effortlessly navigate leaps and climbs with an elegance that often leaves us in awe.
